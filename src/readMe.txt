Data associated with the Coastal Units in the Ebro Delta, Foros Bay, Venice Lagoon, and Vistula Lagoon is published in the following data paper:
C. Arslan, E. Furlan, F. Horneman, A. Stocco, L. Puertolas, N. Alvarez, E. Hineva, N. Andreeva, G. Rozynski & J.M. van Loon-Steensma (2025), Dataset for Physical, Social, and Ecological Characteristics of Coastal Units at four coastal restoration pilots across EU, issue, journal

Data in this publication resulted from the participative application of the NB3 Framework developed as part of Task 4.2 in the Rest-Coast project. For detailed information about the framework, please refer to:
1. Scientific Article: C. Arslan & J.M. van Loon-Steensma (2025), Nature-based building blocks (NB3) framework for scaling up nature-based solutions in coastal adaptation, Nature-Based Solutions, 8, 100259. https://doi.org/https://doi.org/10.1016/j.nbsj.2025.100259
